About

We are JACC Incorporated. During the past couple weeks we have created this company to make life simplier. We are a very diverse company starting in the United States with plans of bring our product to international markets. We are making an innovative, reliable product and providing great service we hope you enjoy.

Mission

Our company JACC Incorporated is developing a portable device that includes a phone charger, Hotspot, and Bluetooth Speaker combined in to what we call '3-in-1.' To help people who spend majority of their time traveling and those who are on their phones a lot. We decided to combine the need for Wifi, adding a Double USB Power Bank, 10000mAh, 1A Output and a speaker to be able to jam to whatever they would like to hear with their portable speaker.
