Mission

My company JACC Incorporated is developing a 3-in-1 Portable Changer, Portable Hotspot, and Bluetooth Speaker combined in to what we call 3N1. To help people who spend majority of their time traveling and those who are on their phones a lot. We decide to combined the need for Wifi you won't be using your data, adding a Double USB Power Bank, 10000mAh, 1A Output and a speaker because everyone want to be able to jam to whatever they would like to hear with their portable speaker.
